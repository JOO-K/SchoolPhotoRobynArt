3D Print & Design Workshop School Photo

This will be your syllabus for this class, 3D Print & Design Workshop. It contains the schedule and what we will be working on that day. The goal of the class is to develop your 3D knowledge, to model your own figure, and to print that figure out in 3D. Lets have fun while designing cool things and learning new skills!

The Theme: School Photo Day Think class portraits — but make them 3D. You'll design and print your own character, rig it with animations, and place it in a virtual school photo setting. Classic poses, custom backdrops, and a final render worthy of the hallway wall.
